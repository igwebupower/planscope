The Problem

Most buyers discover planning issues after making an offer

Conservation areas, refused extensions, restrictive covenants — these details are buried in council websites, taking hours to research. By then, you've already fallen in love with the property.

Features

Data loads in under 2 seconds. No clicking, no searching — it just appears alongside the listing.
