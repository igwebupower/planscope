Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how PlanScope ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website at planscope.vercel.app or use our Chrome extension.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

Similar technologies include:

  • Local Storage: Data stored in your browser that persists until cleared
  • Session Storage: Data stored temporarily for a single browser session

2. How We Use Cookies

We use a minimal number of cookies and similar technologies. We believe in privacy-first design and only collect what is necessary for the Service to function.

2.1 Website Cookies

3. Types of Cookies

3.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be disabled. They include:

  • Cookie consent preferences
  • Security-related cookies

3.2 Analytics Cookies

We currently do not use analytics cookies. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or similar tracking services on our website.

If we introduce analytics in the future, we will update this policy and request your consent.

3.3 Marketing Cookies

We do not use marketing or advertising cookies. We do not track you across websites or serve targeted advertisements.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We do not control cookies set by third parties. When you visit external sites, please review their cookie policies.

Third-party services we link to:

  • Chrome Web Store (Google)
  • PlanIt (planit.org.uk)
  • Planning Data (planning.data.gov.uk)

5. Your Cookie Choices

5.1 Cookie Banner

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie banner asking for your consent. You can:

  • Accept: Allow all cookies
  • Reject: Only essential cookies will be used

5.2 Browser Settings

You can also control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

5.3 Clearing Cookies

To clear cookies already set:

  • Use your browser's "Clear browsing data" feature
  • Select "Cookies and other site data"
  • Choose the time range and clear

Note: Clearing cookies will reset your cookie consent preference, and you'll see the banner again.

5.4 Extension Data

To clear data stored by our extension:

  • Right-click the PlanScope extension icon
  • Select "Options" or "Manage extension"
  • Look for "Clear data" or similar option

Alternatively, removing and reinstalling the extension will clear all stored data.

6. Do Not Track

Some browsers have a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature. Since we do not track users across websites, our response to DNT signals does not change our data practices.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

If we make material changes to how we use cookies, we will notify you through our website or by other means.
