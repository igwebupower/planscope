The data you need, before you need it

Most buyers discover planning issues after making an offer. Conservation areas, Article 4 directions, or a neighbour's refused extension can limit what you can do — and what the property is worth.

PlanScope surfaces this information while you browse, so you can filter out problem properties early and focus on the ones with real potential.

Nearby applications

Every planning application within 500m. Filter by status, type, and date to understand what's happening in the area.

Authority insights

Approval rates and decision timelines for each council. Know whether you're buying in an area that welcomes development.

Constraints flagged

Conservation areas, listed buildings, green belt, flood zones, Article 4 directions. Critical restrictions, surfaced immediately.

Works offline

Data is cached locally. Review properties during viewings, even when mobile signal is unreliable.
