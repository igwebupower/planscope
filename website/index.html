The hidden cost of not knowing

Most property buyers discover planning issues after they've made an offer. Conservation areas, listed building restrictions, or a neighbour's refused extension can significantly impact what you can do with a property — and its value.

Time wasted on properties with restrictive planning histories

Everything you need to know, in one place

PlanScope surfaces the data that matters, right when you need it.

01

Nearby Applications

See every planning application within 500m of the property. Filter by status, type, and date. Understand what developments are happening in the area.

02

Local Authority Insights

Approval rates, average decision times, and planning climate for each council. Know whether you're buying in an area that welcomes development.

03

Planning Constraints

Conservation areas, listed buildings, green belt, flood zones, and Article 4 directions. Critical restrictions flagged before you fall in love with a property.

04

Smart Analysis

Our algorithms identify patterns and flag opportunities. High approval rates for extensions? Many loft conversions nearby? We'll tell you.

05

Works Offline

Data is cached locally so you can review properties even without an internet connection. Perfect for viewings when mobile signal is patchy.

06

Privacy First

We don't track your browsing history or sell your data. The extension only activates on property listing pages. Your searches stay private.
