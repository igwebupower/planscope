Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install PlanScope?

Click "Add to Chrome" on our homepage or visit the Chrome Web Store. The extension will be added to your browser automatically.

Which websites does PlanScope work on?

PlanScope works on Rightmove and Zoopla property listings. Simply visit any property page and the planning data will appear automatically.

Why am I seeing "No planning applications found"?

This means there are no recent planning applications near that property. This is actually good news - it suggests a stable neighbourhood.

How do I upgrade to Pro?

Click "Upgrade" in the extension popup or visit our pricing page. Pro gives you unlimited lookups for £9/month.

How do I activate my licence key?

Open the extension popup, go to the "Licence" tab, and enter your licence key. It will be activated immediately.

Can I get a refund?

Yes, we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. Email us at hello@planscope.uk with your order details.
